Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You will need a valid driver's license, a form of ID, and a credit card for the security deposit.

Is there a minimum age to rent a car?

Yes, you must be at least 21 years old. Additional fees may apply if you're under 25.

Can I return the car after business hours?

Yes, we offer after-hours drop-off at select locations. Contact us in advance to arrange it.

Do you offer insurance?

Yes, we offer various insurance options. Please ask our staff when booking for more details.
