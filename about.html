Who We Are

Established in 2015 with just one vehicle and a big vision, Roman’s Car Rentals has grown into one of Botswana’s most trusted car hire providers. Based in Gaborone, we proudly serve both locals and international travelers with reliable vehicles and excellent service.

From humble beginnings as a family-run business, our fleet now includes over 50 well-maintained cars — from compact options to spacious SUVs. Despite our growth, we’ve stayed true to our roots: personal service, fair pricing, and quality you can count on.

Whether you're commuting, exploring Botswana, or traveling for business, we’re here to make your rental experience smooth, affordable, and stress-free.
